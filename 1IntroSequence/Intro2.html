The Saga of Sigtryggr I

Today is the 27th of Gormánuðr of the year 844, two weeks ago your father, Sigtryggr I the Chief of Meizigarðr,
convened his Viking council and sent forth his best warriors and his youngest son
Sigtryggr I, you, to raze the rich "holy" island of Lindisfarne. The northern sea had other plans for your
expedition and sent it sprawling thousands of miles off course, losing half the fleet in the process.
